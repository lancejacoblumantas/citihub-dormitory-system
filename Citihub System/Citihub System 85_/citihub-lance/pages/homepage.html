House Rules & Policies

Living together,
respectfully

Our rules are designed to create a safe, peaceful, and orderly environment for all residents. All tenants are expected to follow these policies.

1
Keep Doors Closed
Please keep doors closed at all times for privacy and security of all residents.
2
Visitor Policy
Visitors are not allowed to visit other tenants' rooms. Visitors must remain in designated common areas only.
3
No Sharing of Beds
The use of extra beds and lockers is not allowed. Each bed space is for one registered resident only.
4
Smoking in Designated Areas Only
Smoking is only allowed in designated areas. Smoking inside rooms or common spaces is strictly prohibited.
5
Dress Code in Shared Spaces
As a respect to others, appropriate clothing or towel covering is not allowed in shared spaces after bathing.
6
No Visitors Inside Rooms
For security reasons, visitors are not allowed inside the dormitory rooms at any time.
7
No Hanging in Common Areas
Hanging of clothes and other items in shared spaces is not allowed to keep communal areas orderly.
8
Noise & Conduct
As a respect to other tenants, please avoid making loud noises. Bullying, harassment, discrimination, and foul words are strictly prohibited.
9
No Alcohol or Illegal Substances
To avoid unwanted incidents, drinking and promoting alcoholic beverages are not allowed. Illegal drugs, deadly weapons, gambling, and fighting will result in IMMEDIATE TERMINATION.
10
No Personal Cooking
For fire safety concerns, personal cooking is not allowed inside the dormitory rooms or common areas.
11
No Pets
Pets are not allowed except for CitiHub residents' approved pets. Please check with management for details.
12
Allowed Appliances Only
High-wattage appliances such as hot water kettle, rice cooker, hair dryer, and similar items are not allowed. Laptops, smartphones, and tablets are fine.
Penalties
1st Offense Verbal warning with memo.
2nd Offense Memo and 200 penalty fee.
3rd Offense Ban and contract termination.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your stay at CitiHub.

What are the requirements to make a booking?
Your safety is important to us. That's why we require a valid government ID, company ID, and NBI / Barangay Clearance for everyone who stays in CitiHub.
Can I book in advance?
Yes. You can make a reservation up to six months in advance. Once you pay your reservation fee, your bed will be reserved.
Do you accept walk-ins?
Sure. Our resident manager will assist you, Tuesdays to Saturdays 9am to 6pm.
How long can I stay?
Each booking can be a maximum of five months. If you want to stay longer, or if you want to extend your stay, you just need to make another booking.
What happens if I need to cancel my reservation?
You can cancel your booking up to seven days before your check-in date. Your reservation fee minus 500 will be refunded.
Can I cancel my booking once it's started?
Yes, but your deposit and the remaining advance will be forfeited.
Can I visit the site?
Our office is open from Tuesday to Saturday, 9am to 6pm. In case you want to visit the site outside office hours, you can ask for assistance from our security personnel.
What time can I check in?
Check-in is allowed between 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM only. Our resident manager or security personnel will assist you.
How do I pay?
When you need to pay for your reservation fee or monthly rent, we'll send you a text/email with a link to pay. Payment is done through the Collo App (GCash, Bank Apps, etc.).
What happens if I can't pay on time?
We understand that sometimes it's hard to make rent, but we have to make sure everybody pays their rent on time. We will charge a 20% overdue penalty on late payments.
Is there a parking for lease?
Yes, we provide parking slots for motorcycles and cars as an add-on. Parking availability may differ between sites.
Can I invite somebody over?
To ensure the privacy and safety of our tenants, visitors are not allowed inside the premises.
Am I allowed to bring appliances?
No, appliances are not allowed inside the premises.
Can I transfer to a different bed?
We allow you to choose your bed at check-in, but if after that you decide that you want to move, bed transfers cost 200. This is to pay for the overhead incurred with the scheduling changes.
Who is responsible for cleaning the beds and common area?
Please practice CLAYGO (Clean As You Go) inside CitiHub. You're responsible for making sure that your own bed is neat and tidy, but the maintenance staff will handle the common areas (hallways, showers, etc.).
Is there a tenant curfew?
No, there is no tenant curfew.
Do you have a female dormitory?
Unfortunately, we don't have any female dormitories yet. But we're looking into opening one in the future. Stay tuned!
Why is there an age limit?
Unfortunately, we can only accept minors and senior citizens on a case-by-case basis. Our dormitories are very vertical with our bunk beds and three floors which makes them hard to navigate for the elderly. If you still want to stay with us, please contact us directly.
