Our Story

From a small boarding
house to a community

CitiHub Dormitory started in 2018 as a modest boarding house on 1st Street, Santa Mesa - just a few rooms, a handful of students, and a big dream of making student housing better in Manila.

Over the years, we expanded our facilities, improved our services, and grew our community to accommodate hundreds of residents. Today, CitiHub is more than just a dormitory - it's a community where everyone support each other, grow together, and thrive.

We remain committed to our founding principle: everyone deserves a safe, clean, and affordable place to call home.

Mission, Vision & Values

The principles that guide everything we do at CitiHub Dormitory.

Mission
What we do
To provide safe, clean, affordable, and comfortable dormitory living for everyone and young professionals in Manila - enabling them to focus on their studies and career growth without worrying about housing.
Vision
Where we're going
To be the most trusted and preferred dormitory community in Metro Manila - a place where every resident feels welcomed, secure, and empowered to reach their full potential.
Values
What we stand for
  • Safety First - your security is our top priority
  • Community - building bonds beyond roommates
  • Integrity - honest, transparent management
  • Affordability - quality living at fair prices
  • Excellence - maintaining the highest standards
Our Journey

How CitiHub grew

2018
CitiHub Founded
Opened our doors with 2 room types and 44 bed spaces on 1st Street, Santa Mesa. Our very first 20 residents move in.
2019
Expansion Phase 1
Renovated and expanded to accommodate 150 residents. Added Premium Air-Conditioned rooms and upgraded bathroom facilities.
2021
Fiber-Optic WiFi Upgrade
Upgraded internet infrastructure to fiber-optic, supporting online learning needs during and after the pandemic. All floors connected.
2022
Full Capacity Expansion
Reached full 396-bed capacity with improved security systems, CCTV cameras, and upgraded common areas including the dining and study lounge.

Vision
To eradicate homelessness.

We envision a Metro Manila where every student and working professional has access to safe, clean, and dignified housing - because shelter is a right, not a privilege.

Mission
To be a for-profit social enterprise that provides affordable and dignified living spaces with low environmental footprint for the low income workers.

We achieve this through sustainable, eco-friendly construction practices - including dormitories built with recycled shipping containers - keeping costs low while maintaining quality.
