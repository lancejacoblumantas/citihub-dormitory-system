Terms & Privacy Consent

Welcome to CitiHub Dormitory. By proceeding, you agree to the following house rules and data privacy policies:

I. DORMITORY HOUSE RULES
  • Keep Doors Closed: Maintain privacy and security at all times.
  • Visitor Policy: Visitors stay in designated common areas only; not allowed in tenant rooms.
  • No Sharing of Beds: One bed space per registered resident only.
  • Smoking: Strictly prohibited inside rooms or common spaces.
  • Dress Code: Proper clothing/towel covering required in shared spaces.
  • Noise & Conduct: No loud noises. Bullying, harassment, or foul words are strictly prohibited.
  • No Hanging: Do not hang clothes in common areas to keep them orderly.
  • Prohibited Substances: No alcohol, illegal drugs, or weapons. Violation results in IMMEDIATE TERMINATION.
  • No Personal Cooking: Not allowed inside rooms or common areas for fire safety.
  • No Pets: Only CitiHub-approved pets are allowed.
  • Allowed Appliances: Laptops/phones/tablets only. High-wattage items (kettles, rice cookers) are prohibited.
II. DATA PRIVACY CONSENT (RA 10173)

We collect your personal info and residency requirements to process your application and send billing notifications. Your data is stored securely in our database and will be handled according to the Data Privacy Act of 2012.
