I

Booking and Requirements

1
Mandatory Documentation

Employed residents may provide a Company ID or COE. Unemployed residents must provide a valid Gov't ID, Medical Certificate, and NBI Clearance. Students may provide a School ID.

2
Check-in

Allowed only between 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM. Residents must only bring belongings that fit within the provided bed/storage space.

3
Cancellations

Cancellations made up to 7 days before check-in are eligible for a refund (Reservation Fee minus 500).

Walk-ins or same-day check-in cancellations are non-refundable.
II

Payment Terms & Financial Policies

1
Billing Cycle

Invoices are issued 5 days before the due date (the 1st of every month) via the Collo App, Email, and Viber.

2
Transaction Fees

A 30 fee applies to all payments made via QR Scan (GCash, Bank Apps, etc.).

3
Late Penalties

A 20% penalty is applied starting on the 4th day after the due date.

4
Grace Period & Termination

A 15-day grace period is provided. Failure to pay within this period results in Contract Termination.

Non-payment within the grace period will result in immediate contract termination.
5
Renewal

Residents with three (3) late payments will not be eligible for contract renewal.

III

Monthly Add-Ons and Transfers

-
Monthly Add-Ons

Charges for Parking (Car: 4,000; Motor: 600), Lockers (200-450), and Wi-Fi (200) are automatic and can only be removed upon contract renewal.

Car Parking - 4,000
Motor Parking - 600
Locker - 200-450
Wi-Fi - 200
-
Transfers / Upgrades

A 200 service fee applies for bed transfers or contract upgrades (e.g., Fan to A/C), plus any prorated rate differences.

IV

House Rules and Safety

1
Prohibited Appliances

High-consumption items (fans, heaters, rice cookers, hair dryers, etc.) are strictly prohibited. Unauthorized items will be confiscated by Admin.

Only laptops, phones, and tablets are permitted at charging stations.
2
Code of Conduct

Residents must adhere to the visitor policy (common areas only), dress code, and noise restrictions.

3
Zero Tolerance
Use of alcohol, illegal drugs, or weapons results in IMMEDIATE TERMINATION.
V

Data Privacy Consent (RA 10173)

By proceeding, you consent to CitiHub Dormitory collecting and storing your personal data and residency requirements securely for application processing and billing notifications.

This is in compliance with Republic Act No. 10173, also known as the Data Privacy Act of 2012. Your information will never be shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
